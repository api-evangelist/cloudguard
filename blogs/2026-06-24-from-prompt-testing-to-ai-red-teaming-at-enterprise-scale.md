Anyone can try to break a chatbot. That is part of what makes AI red teaming feel accessible. Open a model, write a strange prompt, ask for something the system should refuse, reframe the request, and see what happens. Sometimes the result is funny. Sometimes it is useful. Sometimes it is a little alarming. As a starting point, that kind of testing has value. It is not enough for enterprise AI....
